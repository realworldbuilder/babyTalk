Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2025

At BabyOps, we understand that your family's privacy is paramount. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, how it's used, and how we protect it.

Privacy at a Glance

  • Voice recordings: Sent to OpenAI for transcription, then deleted
  • Baby data: Stored only on your device, never shared
  • No analytics: We don't track your usage or behavior
  • No ads: We don't show ads or sell your data to advertisers
  • No social features: We don't share your data with other users

Information We Collect

Voice Recordings

When you use the voice logging feature, we temporarily collect audio recordings of your spoken entries (e.g., "Fed Emma at 2am, bottle, 4 ounces"). These recordings are:

  • Sent securely to OpenAI's Whisper service for speech-to-text transcription
  • Processed to extract feeding, sleep, and care information
  • Deleted immediately after transcription is complete
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Only used for the specific purpose of creating your baby log entries

Transcribed Text

The text version of your voice recordings is sent to OpenAI's GPT-4o to structure the information into organized log entries. This text may include:

  • Times and descriptions of feedings, sleep, diaper changes
  • Amounts (ounces, duration)
  • Notes and observations about your baby
  • Names or identifiers you mention

This text is processed solely to create structured log entries and is not stored by us or used for any other purpose.

Baby Profile Information

Information you enter about your baby (name, birth date, etc.) is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Only accessible to you and other users you explicitly share your device with

Historical Log Data

Your baby's daily logs, patterns, and historical tracking data are:

  • Stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure data storage
  • Never uploaded to cloud services or external servers
  • Only accessible through the BabyOps app on your device

Technical Information

We collect minimal technical information necessary for the app to function:

  • iOS version (to ensure compatibility)
  • App version (for troubleshooting)
  • Error logs (only when crashes occur, and only if you choose to send them)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos (unless you explicitly attach them to entries)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Social media accounts or profiles

How We Use Your Information

Voice Processing

Voice recordings are used exclusively to:

  • Convert speech to text using OpenAI's Whisper service
  • Structure information into baby log entries using OpenAI's GPT-4o
  • Improve transcription accuracy for future entries

App Functionality

Your local data is used to:

  • Display daily timelines and feeding schedules
  • Generate insights and patterns about your baby's routines
  • Provide AI-powered responses to your baby care questions
  • Export summaries for healthcare providers

Technical Support

If you contact us for support, we may use provided information to:

  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Answer questions about app functionality
  • Provide customer support

Information Sharing and Third Parties

OpenAI Services

We use OpenAI's services for voice processing:

  • OpenAI Whisper: Converts your voice recordings to text
  • OpenAI GPT-4o: Structures transcribed text into organized log entries
  • OpenAI's Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

OpenAI's data processing is governed by their privacy policy. Audio recordings and text are processed according to their enterprise data processing agreements and are not used to train their models.

No Other Third Parties

We do not share your information with any other third parties, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Social media platforms
  • Healthcare providers (unless you explicitly share data with them)
  • Other parents or users

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only if required by law, such as:

  • Valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

Data Security

Local Storage Protection

  • Baby profile and log data is stored using iOS's encrypted data protection
  • Data is protected by your device's passcode or biometric authentication
  • Information is isolated within the BabyOps app sandbox

Transmission Security

  • Voice recordings are transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • All communications with OpenAI services use secure, encrypted connections
  • No data is stored in transit or on intermediate servers

Access Controls

  • Only you can access your baby's data through the app on your device
  • No BabyOps employees have access to your personal data
  • Data cannot be accessed remotely by our systems

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any log entries
  • Export: You can export your data for your own use
  • Delete: You can delete all data by removing the app

Voice Processing

  • You can use the app without voice features by entering data manually
  • You control when voice recordings are made
  • You can review and edit all transcribed content before saving

Communication

  • We don't send marketing emails or notifications
  • You can contact us to ask questions about your data
  • You can request information about data processing

Children's Privacy

BabyOps is designed for parents and caregivers to track information about their babies and children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

The app is intended for use by parents and caregivers who are responsible for the information they enter about their children. Parents have full control over what information is recorded and how it's used.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • We'll notify users through the app if there are significant changes
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy
